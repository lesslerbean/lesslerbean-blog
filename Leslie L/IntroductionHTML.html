Introduction

Hey everyone! My name is Leslie Lovell. I'm seventeen years old and I am making this blog for a couple of reasons. First off, I needed a place to escape to and to share my thoughts freely. Secondly, I had to do it as a school assignment. It fit my purposes. And finally, it just seems like fun.

Okay, so a little bit about the site. I've got a couple different things going on. This, obviously, is the intro. It says at the top, for anyone who didn't notice. The other pages will include a journal of sorts, for all of you who are bored and want to know what goes on in my day to day life. It will probably be relatively private on here, and by "private" I mean that my immediate family won't be snooping around online to see if they can hunt down my digital journal. At least I hope not. Ah, I'm rambing. Oops. That happens more often than I'd like to admit, unfortunately. Anyway, the other pages, aside from the journal, will include a collection of recipes, a collection of DIY crafts I intend to undertake (along with information about how they turn out when I do), my experiences with coding, what I'm learning in school (which will be separate from my life journal because I want to keep my school life as far away from my home life as possible), an area for stress relief, and a collection of my various writings so I can get feedback from you guys. I'll update this if I decide to add more pages.
